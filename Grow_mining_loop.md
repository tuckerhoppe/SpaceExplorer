# Mining Growing Loop

Changing the game loop to be more geared towards mining, and the ship growing. 

## 1. Cargo Limit/Updated Station Purpose
The player can only hold a certain amount of gems in their cargo hold at a time. To facilitate this, we need two places that gems are stored: the cargo hold, and the Gem Vault(Or some other name). We need a clear HUD element to illustrate this. When the Cargo hold is approaching full, it should turn yellow or orange to show caution. When it is full, it should turn red. The player can no longer pick up gems,  The player can drop off their gems at any space station to empty their cargo and move their gems to the gem vault. The Gem Vault can hold an infinite number. 

Dying removes half of the player's gems in the CARGO HOLD. it does not affect gems in the gem vault.

The player's spawn point is the most recent space station they docked at.

## 2. Mining and region Refocus:
Each region has not only more difficult enemies, but larger asteroids. These Larger Asteroids are worth more gems and harder to destroy. 
In order to facilitate this, the larger asteroids will start dropping red and purple gems. Red are worth 2, and Purple are worth 5. That way visually the player can tell that they are earning a lot more gems, but the game doesn't need to change the math too muchfor how many gems are dropped.

The size/type of gems spawned in other regions are determined by the difficulty of the region. For example Level 1 regions have only small asteroids, Level 2 have slightly more difficult to destroy asteroids that give slightly more gems. Level three starts spawning larger asteroids that drop red gems.

Level 12 asteroids should be STAGGERINGLY Big. And so each difficulty level leading up to level 12 should have asteroids that are progressively larger. 
Even though we are messing with the size of the asteroids, each asteroid size should not be uniform. Consider the way that asteroids spawn in the game now, there is a range of sizes. Each level of asteroid the range of sizes just moves up.

The player can still venture into other regions, but it will be very difficult to survive and to actually destroy the asteroids to get the gems.

Each region should have at least a few of each kind of Asteroids. More difficult regions should still have a majority of their level of asteroids, but a few small ones to remember the difference in Scale.

Each Region should display its difficulty level in the Region title along with a small difficulty warning. If the difficulty level is past what is recommended by the above list, it should be red and give a small warning. If it is just one above the base level(for example level two for starter rover) it should be yellow, and if it is at the exact level(like level 7 for the milenium Falcon) it should be green


## 3. Growing:
In order to keep up with each region the player must buy new ships. 

Each new ship bought will increase the size of the ship. The Camera will also zoom out a little bit. So visually it will look bigger in the center of the screen, but the camera will zoom out enough so that it looks just slightly bigger.

Level 12 asteroids are STAGGERINGLY Big. In a level 1 ship like the starter rover, a level 12 asteroid would take up a large chunk of the screen, but by the time the ship is upgraded far enough, like to the Federation Starship, its won't take up nearly as much space.

This way there is a clear visual progression in the player's progress.

## Balancing
Right now there are 6 ships. Each ship should be sufficient for 2 levels. For example:
-Starter Rover: Sufficient for level 1 and 2 regions. Starting at 3 it gets pretty difficult to manage.
-Astro Cruiser: Level 3 & 4. Starts getting difficult at level 5 regions
-Galaxy Voyager: Level 5 & 6. Starts getting difficult at level 7 regions
-Milenium Falcon: Level 7 & 8. Starts getting difficult at level 9 regions
-Klingon Cruiser: Level 9 & 10. Starts getting difficult at level 11 regions
-Federation Starship: Level 11 & 12




## Simplify Science:
We are also going to simplify the Science Points System. Science will be primarily earned from discovering new stellar objects, a completing a region(finding all stellar objects in a region), or quests rather than doing the science mini-game.

As a result, we need to increase how much science is earned from these activities. 

## Simplify Quests: 
The main purpose for quests is earning science points. Rather than Quests being the main purpose of the game, they are fun side quests that help facilitate exploring the galaxy and getting science points.
